What is C Tracker?

C-Tracker is an open-source tool developed by Phaethon Technologies to aid the global effort to contain Covid 19. The Biggest challenge with the Covid 19 is the prevention of Community spread of the disease. C-tracker is designed to be the last stand and the state of the art tool in the arsenal of authorities to continue contact tracing and prevent community spread to the bay.

How it works?

C-Tracker is a Geo Spatial tool that make use of the power of data analytics. Ctracker analyzes your chances of getting infected with CoVid-19 by analyzing the location history of your mobile phones to that of declared hot zones. These hot zones are marked based on the available data from authorites.
